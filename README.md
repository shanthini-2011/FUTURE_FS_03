## 💼 Business Pitch Explanation
I have designed and developed a professional, interactive website for a local business, "Shanthu Cafe," to help them transition from a physical storefront to a 24/7 premium digital presence. 

**How this website helps the business grow:**
1. **Wider Reach & 24/7 Orders:** The website allows customers to explore the menu and place custom cake orders anytime, directly increasing sales opportunities beyond regular shop hours.
2. **Premium Customer Experience:** By integrating modern web features like Dual Theme (Dark/Light mode), Lo-fi Background Music, and an "AI Cake Matchmaker", the website provides an engaging, high-end experience that builds strong brand loyalty.
3. **Operational Efficiency:** The automated "Custom Cake Order" form with a simulated Live Tracking UI reduces the time staff spend manually taking and explaining orders over the phone, streamlining the business workflow.

Overall, this custom-built (HTML/CSS/JS) digital storefront solves the problem of limited local reach and positions Shanthu Cafe for scalable online growth.

## 🚀 Features Built
* 🌓 **Dual Theme:** Custom Light and Dark mode toggle functionality.
* 🤖 **AI Cake Recommender:** A dynamic JavaScript logic that suggests cakes based on user mood.
* 🛒 **Interactive UI:** Animated "Add to Cart" sparkles and magic cursor trail.
* 📍 **Order Tracking:** A simulated step-by-step progress bar for the order form.
* 🎵 **Immersive Experience:** Integrated Lo-fi cafe background music.
